Mending Stones is a website currently under development, built for the purpose of viewing and interacting with a rotoscoping project for The Yale School of Art
Experimental Animation class (ART 285) by MFA candidate Fatima Al-Kuwari. For the many ways we mend ourselves, each with our own stories. This is about grief.
